INTERNFLOW SAMPLE NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)

This Sample NDA is provided for UI demonstration and testing purposes.
It is NOT a legally binding agreement.

1. Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes non-public business, technical, product, process,
customer, and operational details shared during internship onboarding and execution.

2. Non-Disclosure Obligation
The Receiving Party agrees to:
- keep Confidential Information strictly confidential,
- use Confidential Information only for authorized internship activities,
- not disclose Confidential Information to any unauthorized third party.

3. Permitted Access
Access is limited to personnel who need the information to perform assigned responsibilities
and who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

4. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that:
- is publicly available without breach,
- was already known lawfully,
- is independently developed without use of confidential material,
- is lawfully received from a third party without restriction.

5. Term
Confidentiality obligations survive internship completion for the period required by policy
or applicable law.

6. Return/Deletion
Upon request or completion, Confidential Information and derivative copies must be returned
or securely deleted, except where retention is required by law.

7. No License
No intellectual property rights are granted by disclosure of Confidential Information.

8. Governing Policy
This sample document follows internal policy placeholders and is only for product demo use.

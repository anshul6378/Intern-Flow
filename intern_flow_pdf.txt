Business Requirements Document (BRD) 
Application Name: Intern Flow 
Internship Program Automation (Unpaid Internships) 
Version: 1.0 
 
1. Executive Summary 
Intern Flow is a centralized, workflow-driven platform designed to digitize, standardize, 
and govern the end-to-end Unpaid Internship Program. 
The system replaces fragmented email- and chat-based coordination with a secure, 
auditable, AI-assisted portal that spans referral intake, candidate evaluation, 
onboarding, access provisioning, internship lifecycle management, and closure. 
The primary aim is to ensure speed, compliance, transparency, and accountability, 
while significantly reducing manual coordination across Referrers, Mentors, HR, 
Admin/Security, IT (AD), and Compliance teams. 
 
2. Objectives & Success Criteria 
2.1 Business Objectives 
1. Digitize referral intake through a standardized internal portal. 
2. Automate inter-department handoffs and notifications. 
3. Leverage AI to:  
o Parse resumes and prefill data 
o Validate eligibility and readiness 
o Detect duplicates and missing information 
o Draft contextual communications 
4. Ensure timely creation of Non-Worker IDs, NDA execution, and credential 
provisioning. 
5. Provide dashboards, SLA tracking, audit trails, and compliance reporting. 
 
2.2 Success Measures

3. Scope 
3.1 In Scope 
• Internship referral intake portal 
• Internship Referral Form automation 
• AI-assisted resume parsing, deduplication, and prefill 
• Automated notifications, tasks, reminders, and escalations 
• Digital Joining Form with validations 
• Non-Worker ID creation workflow 
• NDA issuance via e-sign and archival 
• Offer/confirmation letter generation 
• Internship lifecycle tracking (start, delay, extension, closure) 
• Certificate request and issuance 
 
3.2 Out of Scope (Phase 1) 
• Stipend or payroll processing 
• Advanced intern performance appraisal 
• LMS or external university system integrations 
• Stipend compliance or financial modules

5. Current-State (As-Is) Summary 
The current process relies on: 
• Informal referrals via email or Teams 
• Manual document exchange 
• Ad-hoc follow-ups for Non-Worker IDs, NDAs, and access 
• Minimal audit trail and inconsistent SLA adherence 
This leads to delays, compliance risk, duplicated effort, and poor visibility. 
 
6. Future-State (To-Be) with AI Enablement 
• Portal-based referral submission with validations 
• AI-powered resume parsing and duplicate detection 
• Workflow-driven task assignment across HR, IT, Admin 
• Event-based email notifications and reminders 
• Central dashboard for SLA, bottleneck, and compliance tracking 
• Full audit trail across the internship lifecycle

7. Functional Requirements 
7.1 Referral Intake Portal 
FR-1: Employees shall submit referrals exclusively through the Intern Flow portal. 
Acceptance: Portal accessible via intranet; submissions logged with timestamps. 
 
7.2 Internship Referral Form 
FR-2: Capture all mandatory values (Section 9.1) with format validation. 
Acceptance: Submission blocked until mandatory fields completed. 
 
7.3 AI Resume Parsing & Prefill 
FR-3: 
• Extract candidate name, email, phone, education, skills 
• Prefill referral form 
• Confidence scoring with human override 
Acceptance: ≥ 90% accuracy on basic fields; override tracked. 
 
7.4 Eligibility & Readiness Checks 
FR-4: 
• Confirm unpaid consent 
• Confirm in-person readiness 
• Validate joining location alignment 
Acceptance: Submission blocked if readiness flags not confirmed. 
 
7.5 Joining & Onboarding 
FR-6: Digital joining form with save-draft, validations, attachments, and HR lock. 
FR-7: Auto-trigger Non-Worker ID task on joining form completion (SLA 1 day). 
FR-8: NDA issued via e-sign; start blocked until signed.

9. Data Model – Forms & Fields 
9.1 Internship Referral Form 
(All fields mandatory unless configured optional) 
• Candidate details 
• Eligibility confirmations (unpaid, in-person) 
• Referrer & Mentor details 
• Internship duration, dates, project overview 
• Relationship declaration 
(Field list preserved exactly as supplied, with validations applied consistently.) 
 
9.2 Joining Form 
Includes: 
• Personal details 
• Employment identifiers (Non-Worker ID) 
• Address & emergency contact 
• Education & employment history 
• Government IDs 
• Declaration & signature 
 
9.3 Certificate Request Form 
Captured post-completion for certificate issuance.
